## FAQ

Q1: How does Credex find savings without asking for my invoices?

A1: Credex asks you to list the AI tools, plans, team size, and primary use cases — we analyze that input, apply proven heuristics and pricing data, then surface defensible recommendations. You can optionally store results in Supabase; we never require billing credentials.

Q2: Will using Credex interrupt my team's tools or users?

A2: No. Credex only analyzes and suggests changes — it won't modify any accounts or billing. Recommendations are actionable steps you (or your admin) can perform in the vendor console.

Q3: Is my data private? Where is it stored?

A3: By default, audits are stored only in your browser. If you choose to save an audit for sharing, we store a redacted, public-friendly copy in our database (no emails shown unless you opt into lead capture). Sensitive keys must never be provided.

Q4: How accurate are the savings estimates?

A4: Estimates are based on vendor pricing and the inputs you provide (plan, seats, usage). We surface conservative, defensible savings and label “quick wins” versus longer-term opportunities. Use our audit as a negotiation and planning starting point.

Q5: What if I need help implementing recommendations?

A5: After the audit you can book a 15-minute consultation with our team for a technical review and implementation plan; high-savings audits are prioritized for outreach.
